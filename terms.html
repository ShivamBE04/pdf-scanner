Push Notifications & Firebase Cloud Messaging

If you enable push notifications, the app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send notifications to your device. When you enable notifications:

  • An FCM token is generated on your device
  • The token is sent to Firebase servers to enable message delivery
  • Firebase may associate the token with your device and Advertising ID

You can disable notifications at any time:

  • Android: App Settings → Notifications → Toggle off
  • Or: Device Settings → Apps → PDF Scanner → Notifications → Disable

Disabling notifications does not affect other app features. Your FCM token is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app or disable notifications.
